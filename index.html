Q & A

Q:What should I wear?

A: Anything in the business casual to semi-formal range works beautifully. No need to match the theme. We do have one small request: dark blue is the groom's color and white is the bride's, so we're saving those shades for them.

Q: Where is the the reception?

A: The reception will be held in the same building/room!

Q: Who can I bring with me?

A: To keep the wedding at capacity, we have attached plus ones to your invitation if you were allowed one. Otherwise, only the names listed on the invitation are to come.

Q: Are children welcome?

A: Due to damage liability and other concerns, we are limiting children to nieces and nephews only unless otherwise discussed with the bride or groom.

Q: Will there be alcohol?

A: There will be no alcohol served at this wedding.

Q: What's for dinner?

A: Chicken Breast with House Spice Rub, then grilled over an open flame with roasted red potatoes and a roasted vegetable medley. This will be served with a garden salad, dinner roll, assorted desserts, coffee, iced tea, and water.

Q: When should I RSVP by?

A: Please RSVP by March 31st
